Omnidirectional path finding algorithm:

An omnidirectional pathfinding algorithm with a single ultrasonic sensor enables a robot to detect obstacles in all directions by rotating the sensor or the robot itself. The sensor measures distance at multiple angles, creating a 360-degree map of the surroundings. Based on these readings, the algorithm identifies the clearest direction and computes a path to avoid obstacles. This approach is cost-effective and simple but may be slower than systems with multiple sensors due to the need for continuous scanning.
